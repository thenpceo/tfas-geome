Illuminate Your Purpose

I help people feel better in their body and mind through personalized bodywork, yoga, and meditation.

I'm a wellness practitioner blending massage, Thai bodywork, yoga, and meditation to help people slow down, release tension, and return to a state of ease. Every session is tailored to your body and goals — supporting strength, mobility, and nervous system regulation.

Whether you need deep tension relief, guided movement, or a full immersive reset, I meet you where you are and help your body find its way back to balance.
